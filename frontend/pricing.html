Frequently Asked Questions

What happens to my pins when I upgrade?

All your existing pins are preserved. When you upgrade to Pro, they'll automatically sync across your Chrome devices.

Is this a one-time payment?

Yes! Pro is a one-time payment of $9.99 for lifetime access. Premium will be a subscription ($99/year or $199 lifetime).

What AI platforms will be supported?

Currently we support ChatGPT. We're actively developing support for Claude, Gemini, and Grok. Pro users will get automatic access when these launch.

Can I get a refund?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact support@pinboard-gpt.dps.codes for a full refund.

What's the difference between Chrome sync and Cloud sync?

Chrome sync uses browser storage (limited to 100KB) and works only across Chrome devices. Premium's cloud sync has unlimited storage and works across all browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari).
