Privacy Policy

Your data stays local - no accounts, no tracking, no external servers.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Pinboard GPT is built with privacy as a core principle. Your data is yours, stored locally on your device, and never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

What Data We Collect

Pinboard GPT recognizes two separate collection models:

  1. Local / browser-managed data (Free & Pro): For Free users (up to 10 pins) and Pro users (when using browser sync), all pin data remains under your control — stored locally in your browser (IndexedDB) or encrypted in your browser's sync service (Chrome Sync / Firefox Sync). We do not collect or transmit this data to our servers for Free or Pro users by default.
  2. Optional cloud collection (Premium, opt-in): If you explicitly opt into Premium cloud sync, we will securely store the following data on our encrypted backend to enable cross-browser sync and larger storage limits:
    • ChatGPT message content that you choose to pin
    • Custom names and tags you add to pins
    • URLs of ChatGPT conversations for navigation
    • Timestamps of when pins are created

By default, new users are on the Free tier and their data remains local. Pro users who enable browser sync still keep data managed by the browser provider — we only receive or store pin data if you explicitly enable Premium cloud sync.

What We Don't Do

  • ❌ No analytics or tracking
  • ❌ No data transmission to external servers by default
  • ❌ No third-party SDKs for data collection
  • ❌ No cookies for tracking (only browser-native storage):
  • ❌ No advertising or hidden monetization
Note on Accounts & Authentication:

Although the extension requires no account for basic use, signing in is required when you choose to purchase Pro/Premium or enable cloud sync. Authentication is optional and used only to:

  • Authorize purchases and associate licenses with your account (we use industry-standard OAuth providers such as Google for login).
  • Enable browser sync (Pro) or optional encrypted cloud sync (Premium) when you explicitly opt in.
  • Store minimal profile data (email and display name) and secure tokens required for licensing and sync — we do not collect or store additional personal data without your consent.

In short: basic use remains account-free and local-first. Accounts and authentication are only introduced when you opt into paid features or cross-device sync. We strive to minimize data collection and to make what is stored and why fully transparent.

Permissions Explained

Pinboard GPT requests the following browser permissions:

Storage

Why: To save your pins locally in IndexedDB.

Data: Only pin data you explicitly create.

Context Menus

Why: To add the right-click "Pin selection" option.

Data: Only text you select and choose to pin.

Active Tab & Tabs

Why: To navigate to pinned messages and highlight them in context.

Data: No data collected; only used for navigation.

Host Permissions (chatgpt.com, chat.openai.com)

Why: To interact with ChatGPT pages for pinning and highlighting.

Data: Only messages you choose to pin.

Your Data Rights

  • Full Control: You can export, import, or delete all your pins at any time
  • No Lock-in: Export your data as JSON and use it however you like
  • Complete Removal: Uninstalling the extension removes all local data

Open Source Transparency

Pinboard GPT is fully open source. You can review our code on GitHub to verify our privacy claims. We encourage code audits and welcome security researchers.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We will never make changes that compromise your privacy without clear notification.
