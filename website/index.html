How It Works

Three simple ways to pin messages

1

Hover Method

Move your mouse over any ChatGPT message → Click the "Pin" button that appears

2

Name & Tag

Add a custom name and tags (up to 3) to organize your pins for easy searching

3

Search & Jump

Find pins instantly with search, then click to jump back to the original conversation
