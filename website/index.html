How It Works

Three simple ways to pin messages

1

Pin Messages or Chats

Hover over any message to pin it, or click the "Pin Chat" button to save the entire conversation

2

Name & Tag

Add a custom name, description, and tags (up to 3) to organize your pins for easy searching

3

Search & Jump

Filter by Chats/Messages, search by name or tag, then click to jump back to the original conversation
