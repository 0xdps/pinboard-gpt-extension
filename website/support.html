Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pin a message?

There are three ways to pin messages:

  • Hover Method: Hover over any ChatGPT message and click the "Pin" button
  • Right-Click: Select text, right-click, choose "Pin selection to GPT Pinboard"
  • Quick Pin: Use the "Pin Message" button in the bottom-right corner of ChatGPT

Where is my data stored?

All your pins are stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB. Nothing is sent to external servers. Your data never leaves your device.

Can I export my pins?

Yes! Click the extension icon, then use the Export button to download all your pins as a JSON file. You can import them later or share with others.

The pin button doesn't appear. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  • Refresh the ChatGPT page
  • Make sure the extension is enabled in your browser
  • Check that you're on chatgpt.com or chat.openai.com
  • Try disabling other ChatGPT-related extensions temporarily

How do I search my pins?

Click the extension icon to open your pinboard. Use the search box to filter pins by content, name, or tags. The search is instant and searches across all fields.

Can I sync pins across devices?

Currently, GPT Pinboard stores data locally for privacy. However, you can export your pins from one device and import them on another. We're considering adding optional sync in the future.

Does this work with ChatGPT Plus?

Yes! GPT Pinboard works with both free and Plus accounts. It works with all ChatGPT models including GPT-4 and GPT-4 Turbo.

Troubleshooting

Extension not working after update?

Solution: Try these steps in order:

  1. Refresh the ChatGPT page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
  2. Restart your browser
  3. Disable and re-enable the extension
  4. If issues persist, report on GitHub

Pins not showing up?

Solution: Check your browser's storage settings. Make sure:

  • Cookies and site data are not being cleared automatically
  • The extension has permission to store data
  • Your browser has enough storage space

Can't jump back to pinned message?

Solution: This usually happens if:

  • The original chat was deleted
  • The URL structure changed (rare but possible)
  • You're signed out of ChatGPT

The pin content is still saved - just the navigation link may not work.
