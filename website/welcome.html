Quick Start Guide

Follow these simple steps to start organizing your ChatGPT messages

1

Visit ChatGPT

Go to chatgpt.com or chat.openai.com

The extension only works on official ChatGPT pages

2

Choose Your Pinning Method

Pick the method that works best for you:

  • Hover: Move your mouse over any ChatGPT message → Click "Pin" button
  • Right-Click: Select text → Right-click → "Pin selection to GPT Pinboard"
  • Quick Pin: Use the "Pin Message" button (bottom-right corner)
3

Organize Your Pins

Add custom names and tags to make finding pins easier

Pro tip: Use tags like "code", "study", "work" for quick filtering

4

Access Your Pinboard

Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar to see all your pins

Search, filter, and jump back to original conversations with one click

🔒 Privacy First

All your pins are stored locally on your device. No accounts, no cloud sync, no external servers. Your data is completely private.

Read our Privacy Policy →

💡 Pro Tips

  • Export regularly: Backup your pins to JSON files
  • Use descriptive tags: Makes searching much easier later
  • Pin strategically: Focus on messages you'll want to reference again
  • Keyboard shortcuts: Right-click menu is often the fastest method
