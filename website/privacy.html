Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

TL;DR: GPT Pinboard stores all your data locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or have access to any of your pins or personal information. Your privacy is 100% guaranteed.

Introduction

GPT Pinboard ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our browser extension.

Data Collection & Storage

What Data is Stored

GPT Pinboard stores the following information locally on your device:

  • ChatGPT messages you choose to pin
  • Custom names and tags you add to your pins
  • URLs and metadata to help locate pinned messages
  • Your extension preferences and settings

Where Data is Stored

All data is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB. This means your pins never leave your device and are not sent to any external servers.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not track your browsing history
  • We do not use analytics or telemetry
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • We do not have access to your ChatGPT conversations beyond what you explicitly pin

Browser Permissions Explained

Storage Permission

Required to save your pins locally in IndexedDB. This allows the extension to persist your pins between browser sessions without any external servers.

ActiveTab Permission

Allows the extension to access the currently active ChatGPT tab when you click to open a pin. This is only used to navigate to and highlight your pinned message.

Tabs Permission

Helps locate existing ChatGPT tabs or open new ones when you access a pin. We only interact with tabs you explicitly navigate to through the extension.

ContextMenus Permission

Enables the right-click context menu option to quickly pin selected text from ChatGPT conversations.

Host Permissions (chatgpt.com, chat.openai.com)

Required to interact with ChatGPT pages for pinning messages and highlighting functionality. We only access these domains and only when you initiate actions.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your pins depends on your browser's security and your device's security. We recommend:

  • Using the latest version of your browser
  • Keeping your device secure with passwords and encryption
  • Regularly backing up important pins using the export feature

Data Export & Deletion

Export Your Data

You can export all your pins at any time using the export feature in the extension popup. This creates a JSON file containing all your pins, names, and tags.

Delete Your Data

You can delete individual pins or all data at any time:

  • Individual pins: Click the delete button on any pin
  • All data: Uninstall the extension or clear browser data for the extension

Third-Party Services

GPT Pinboard does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services. The extension operates entirely within your browser and only interacts with ChatGPT pages to facilitate the pinning functionality.

Remote Code

GPT Pinboard does not load or execute any remote code. All scripts and resources are bundled with the extension. No external code is fetched or executed at runtime.

Children's Privacy

GPT Pinboard does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, no age verification is required.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
